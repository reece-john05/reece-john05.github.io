About Tirini

Tirini was born from the travels of Reece John, a San Luis Obispo native who first visited Greece on a summer trip that changed everything. Wandering through the winding streets of Thessaloniki and the whitewashed villages of the Aegean, Reece was struck not only by the flavors of the food but by the spirit behind every meal. From seaside tavernas serving freshly grilled fish to grandmothers offering still-warm spanakopita from their kitchen windows, he discovered a culture where hospitality, simplicity, and connection were at the heart of every dish.

As Reece continued his travels through the Greek islands, he grew fascinated by the way everyday ingredients—olive oil, lemons, oregano, fresh vegetables—could be transformed into meals that were both rustic and deeply flavorful. Inspired by long conversations with local cooks and the warmth of the people he met, he knew he wanted to bring this experience back home. The idea for Tirini began as a notebook filled with recipes, stories, and sketches from his journey—each page capturing the essence of the Greek dining experience.

Returning to San Luis Obispo, Reece saw the perfect opportunity to share what he had learned. Surrounded by rolling hills, local farms, and a community that values fresh, quality ingredients, SLO felt like the ideal place to blend Greek culinary traditions with California’s vibrant food culture. With the support of friends, family, and a small team of passionate cooks, he transformed his vision into Tirini: a welcoming Greek kitchen dedicated to serving authentic flavors with modern, local influence.

Today, Tirini stands as a celebration of Reece’s journey—a tribute to the meals, people, and landscapes that first inspired him. Our mission is simple: to bring the warmth of Greek hospitality and the bright, bold flavors of the Mediterranean to the Central Coast. Whether you're stopping in for a gyro, savoring a plate of lamb chops, or sharing mezze with friends, we invite you to experience a taste of Greece, crafted with the same heart and curiosity that began Reece’s unforgettable adventure.
